⚠ Hazard Warning

Continued high levels of digital engagement may lead to increased dependency on online platforms, where constant interaction becomes a default behaviour rather than a conscious choice.

Over time, this can reduce attention span, make it harder to focus without interruption, and blur the boundary between online and offline identity.

Frequent exposure to notifications, constant content streams, and fast-paced interaction can make your online presence more reactive than reflective, meaning your behaviour may be shaped more by stimulation, algorithms, and validation than by deliberate choice.
