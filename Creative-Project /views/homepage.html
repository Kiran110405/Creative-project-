Digital Doppelgänger

Understand your online behaviour through data visualisation

Digital Doppelgänger is a web app designed to gather information about how you act online and visualise your data in a clear and engaging way. The aim is to help users better understand what they are like online, how their behaviour can be interpreted through data, and how this information can be represented visually as a digital version of themselves.
